### 🔧 From the Field to the Terminal

After 12+ years as a commercial irrigation technician — diagnosing systems, managing sites, and keeping buildings running — I decided to channel that same problem-solving mindset into software development.

I'm building toward a career in **full stack development**, specializing in **Next.js**, **TypeScript**, and **modern SaaS architecture**. My background in physical infrastructure gives me a unique angle on systems thinking — understanding how everything connects and knowing exactly where to look when things break.

Turns out wiring up complex systems and debugging production apps aren't that different. Both require patience, logic, and a solid understanding of how the pieces fit together.
