### 🔧 From the Field to the Terminal

After 12+ years as a commercial irrigation technician — diagnosing systems, managing sites, and keeping buildings running — I decided to channel that same problem-solving mindset into software development.

I'm building toward a career in **backend development**, specializing in **Python**, **FastAPI**, and **REST API design**. My background in physical infrastructure gives me a unique angle on **IoT systems** and building automation — where software meets the real world.

Turns out wiring up sensors and debugging APIs aren't that different. Both require patience, logic, and knowing exactly where to look when things break.
